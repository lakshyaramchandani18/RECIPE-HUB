PROCEDURE

For Cooker Wala Pasta

1.In a pressure cooker, add oil, once it's hot, add ginger, garlic and saute it for a minute.

2.Add onion, butter, salt to taste and saute it until translucent.

3.Add fresh tomato puree and cook for 5-6 minutes on medium flames.

4.Add degi red chili powder, coriander powder and saute it for a minute.

5.Add sugar, sweet corn kernels, tomato, salt to taste, butter, water, tender coriander stems, oregano, chili flakes, penne pasta and mix it well.

6.Cover it with a lid, cook it for a whistle and leave it aside to depressurize the cooker.

7.Finish it with prepared tomato ketchup mixture and give it a good mix.

8.Transfer it to a serving dish, garnish it with grated cheese, dry oregano and basil leaf.

9.Serve hot with prepared garlic bread.
