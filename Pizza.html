PROCEDURE


Making the Dough:

1. In a small bowl, warm the milk and water (not too hot). Add sugar and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.

3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.


Preparing the Sauce and Toppings:

1. In a bowl, mix tomato puree with minced garlic, Italian herbs, and salt to make the pizza sauce.

2. Prepare your toppings by slicing vegetables and cubing paneer.


Assembling and Baking the Pizza:

1. Preheat your oven to 475-500¬∞F (250-260¬∞C).

2. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

3. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.

4. Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

5. Add your toppings evenly over the sauce.

6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and toppings are cooked.

7. Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve hot!
