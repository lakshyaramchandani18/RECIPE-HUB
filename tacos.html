Tacos

INGREDIENTS


Filling: seasoned ground beef, chicken, fish, paneer, or beans

Taco seasoning (cumin, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, pepper)

Shredded lettuce

Chopped onions

Chopped tomatoes

Cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)

Salsa or hot sauce

Sour cream (optional)

Guacamole or sliced avocado (optional)

Italian herbs (oregano, basil)

Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

PROCEDURE


1. Prepare the Filling: In a skillet over medium heat, cook your choice of filling (ground beef, chicken, fish, paneer, or beans) until fully cooked. Add taco seasoning and a little water, simmering until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat.


2. Warm the Tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until they are warm and pliable.


3. Assemble the Tacos: Place a portion of the cooked filling onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, chopped onions, tomatoes, and cheese. Add salsa or hot sauce to taste. Optionally, add a dollop of sour cream and some guacamole or sliced avocado.


4. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle fresh cilantro on top for garnish. Serve the tacos immediately while they are warm and enjoy!
